Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your Privacy is Our Priority

OpenCapture is an offline-first application. We believe your data belongs to you.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data, screen captures, or usage statistics. Everything you capture and edit stays 100% on your local machine.

Network Connectivity

The application works completely offline. It does not make any background network requests to third-party servers for tracking or analytics.

Updates

You may manually visit our GitHub repository to check for and download updates. The application does not auto-update without your permission.

If you have any questions, you can check our GitHub repository.
